Hi friends,

I am excited to invite you to the kick-off meeting for our year-long online study group around The Kybalion by The Three Initiates. We will meet once or twice a month for light reading and real conversation. No pressure to be deep or perfect. Just practical takeaways you can use.

In our first session we will explore the Introduction and Chapter 1, The Hermetic Philosophy. These opening pages set the stage for everything that follows — who the Hermetic teachers were, why these ideas were kept hidden for centuries, and what “The Lips of Wisdom are closed, except to the ears of Understanding” really means.

What to expect:

  • A casual, open conversation — no expertise required.
  • We will read through key passages together and discuss what stands out.
  • Bring any questions, observations, or real-life examples you want to share.

How to prepare:

  1. Read the Introduction and Chapter 1 once through before the meeting.
  2. Highlight or note anything that resonates, confuses, or surprises you.
  3. Think about one situation in your own life where these ideas might apply.
